Clean Bandit

Biography

Clean Bandit is an English electronic music band, formed in Cambridge in 2008. The band consists of Grace Chatto, and brothers Jack and Luke Patterson.

In January 2014, they scored their first UK chart topping single with "Rather Be", a collaboration with Jess Glynne, featuring elements of both classical and dance music.

The song also reached number 10 on the US Billboard Hot 100 which was the beginning of their popularity in the United States.
